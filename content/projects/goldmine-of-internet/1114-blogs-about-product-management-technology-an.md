# Joel on Software: The Archive

This is the historical archive of "Joel on Software," the influential blog by Joel Spolsky. Spolsky is a software developer and the co-founder of successful tech companies like Stack Overflow and Trello.

The archive contains over 1100 articles written over 16 years, from 2000 to 2016. It is considered a goldmine for software developers, product managers, and aspiring founders, covering a wide range of topics in software development and technology management.

The content is organized chronologically, making it a time capsule of the software industry's evolution.

**Source:** [Joel on Software Archive](https://www.joelonsoftware.com/archives/)
